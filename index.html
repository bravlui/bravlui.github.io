About me

Where disciplines
meet and challenge each other

I am a Master's student in Computer Science at São Paulo State University (UNESP) and hold a Bachelor's degree in Statistics and Data Science from the University of São Paulo (USP).

Rather than viewing Artificial Intelligence as a single discipline, I see it as a meeting point between statistics, optimization, networks, information retrieval, and human reasoning.

Can uncertainty improve representation learning?

How can context be incorporated into similarity learning?

Can probabilistic reasoning improve ranking and retrieval?

How can graphs enrich visual and semantic representations?
